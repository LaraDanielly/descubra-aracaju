# Creator context

Use this file with the `short-form-viral`, `travel-creator`, and `creator-monetization` skills.

## Who

- Emerging travel creator discovering **Bahia (Brazil)** for the first time
- Has trip photos and tourist-spot footage from recent travels
- Goal: grow Instagram + TikTok, become a known travel blogger/influencer, eventually monetize
- Languages: communicate plans in **Portuguese (Brazil)** unless they ask otherwise; hooks can be PT-BR first (larger local + BR diaspora reach), with occasional EN tests later

## Current baselines (approx.)

- Instagram: ~700 followers; ~50 people actually interact (likes/stories)
- TikTok: posts get roughly **under 100–200 views**
- Most followers are offline / inactive — growth must come from **non-follower discovery**, not Stories to friends

## Positioning (working)

**One-liner:** “Primeira vez na Bahia, sem filtro de agência — lugares, preços e erros pra você não repetir.”

Niche focus for 90 days: Bahia discovery (Salvador + coast + nature as they visit). Do not dilute into generic “travel everywhere” until one region compounds.

## Constraints

- Small account: prioritize daily/near-daily short video over polished photo carousels
- Reuse existing photo libraries heavily (Ken Burns + text + occasional talking head)
- Honesty over hype: no fake “guaranteed viral” claims
- Related product in this repo: tourism guide app energy (Aracaju / Northeast) — soft bio links only after content earns saves

## Success definition (first 30–60 days)

- TikTok: break past ~200 views consistently; land multiple videos >1k
- Instagram: Reels reaching non-followers; rising saves
- Profile: clear bio + series highlights so a stranger knows what to expect in 3 seconds
- Monetization: **not primary yet** — unlock after repeatable reach

## Content pillars

1. Descoberta (first-timer POV)
2. Armadilha vs lugar bom
3. Quanto custa / como chegar
4. Comida e atmosfera
