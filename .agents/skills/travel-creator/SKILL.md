# Travel Creator Content

Help turn trips and photo libraries into **repeatable short-form content** that strangers want to watch, save, and follow.

## First read context

If `.agents/creator-context.md` exists, read it. Prefer the creator’s real places, language (PT-BR default unless they ask otherwise), and beginner-discovery angle.

## Positioning (Jobs to Be Done)

Viewers don’t follow “someone who traveled.” They follow a job:

- “Show me Bahia like a first-timer who actually figures it out”
- “Tell me what to skip so I don’t waste money”
- “Make me feel the place before I book”

Help the creator pick **one** positioning sentence and stick to it for 90 days.

## Bahia / Northeast angles that travel well

Prioritize specificity over generic “beautiful beach”:

| Angle | Why it works |
|-------|----------------|
| First-time discovery | Relatable; high comment rate |
| Tourist trap vs local pick | Conflict + utility |
| Cost in R$ | Saves; high share to trip groups |
| How to get there (bus/ferry/Uber) | Practical; under-served |
| Food + sound + color | Sensory = retention |
| Safety / timing / crowds | Trust + authority |
| “3 places in one day” | Clear promise, completable |

Cultural respect: don’t exoticize religion, people, or poverty for clicks. Film people with consent. Credit neighborhoods and Black Bahian culture accurately when relevant.

## Turn a photo dump into 10+ videos

For each trip folder, force this extraction:

1. **Hero spot video** — one place, one emotion
2. **Mistake** — what they’d do differently
3. **Price card** — transport + food + entrance
4. **Ranking** — top 3 of the trip
5. **Before/after expectation**
6. **Packing / what to wear**
7. **Food only**
8. **Sunrise/sunset vs midday contrast**
9. **Map / route** (screen + B-roll)
10. **Save-this itinerary** carousel or Reel

Same photos can appear in multiple edits with different hooks.

## Series formats (build habit)

Name series so people recognize them:

- “Bahia em 30 segundos”
- “Não vá em X — vá em Y”
- “Gastei quanto?”
- “Primeiro dia em…”
- “Erro de turista #N”

Series create **mere exposure** and make the profile look intentional.

## Shot / edit guidance from stills

If they only have photos:

- Ken Burns (slow zoom/pan) + text hooks still works on TikTok/IG
- Pair 6–12 photos per 20s video; change every ~1.5s
- Add 1 talking-head clip on phone (even selfie audio) — faces boost trust
- Native captions; large text; high contrast

If they have video: A-roll face for hooks, B-roll for payoff.

## Caption / on-screen text (PT-BR default)

Lead with place + utility:

- “Pelourinho: o que fazer em 2 horas”
- “Morro de São Paulo sem gastar fortuna”
- “Chapada Diamantina: trilha fácil vs difícil”

End CTA: “Salva pra viagem” / “Quer parte 2?” / “Comenta a cidade”

## Cross-link with products (if applicable)

If the workspace has a travel guide site/app (e.g. Destinos no Nordeste / Aracaju guide), mention soft CTAs only after content is earning saves — never let the product kill retention. Bio link comes after the hook earns the profile visit.

## Deliverables when asked for a plan

1. Niche one-liner
2. 7–14 day calendar with place + hook + format
3. Shot list from their existing photos/trips
4. Series names
5. What to film next trip (gap list)
