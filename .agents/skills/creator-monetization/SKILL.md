# Creator Monetization

Money follows **attention + trust + a clear offer**. Don’t sell before the account can reliably reach strangers.

## Stage gate (Theory of Constraints)

| Followers / reach | Focus | Money moves |
|-------------------|-------|-------------|
| <1k or <1k avg views | Hooks, niche, daily shipping | None. Skill only. |
| 1k–10k or posts regularly >5–20k views | Email/WhatsApp list optional; media kit draft | Small local collabs, affiliate links |
| 10k–50k | Rate card, niches brands | Paid Reels, hotels, tourism boards |
| 50k+ | Own product | Guides, courses, membership, agency |

If TikTok is under 200 views, **monetization is not the bottleneck** — packaging is. Fix reach first.

## Ethical order of offers (travel niche)

1. **Free value** that gets saves (itineraries, prices, mistakes)
2. **Affiliate** (booking, experiences, eSIM, tours) — disclose
3. **Local partnerships** (pousadas, restaurants, guides) — barter then paid
4. **Digital product** (“Guia 3 dias em Salvador”, map, PDF)
5. **Retainers** with tourism brands / city marketing
6. **Own community** or trip hosting (advanced)

## Media kit essentials (when ready)

- Niche one-liner
- Audience location + language
- Best 3 video screenshots (views)
- Engagement rate on top posts (not vanity average)
- Past collabs / samples
- Clear packages: 1 Reel, Reel+Stories, usage rights

## Pricing intuition (Brazil travel, rough)

Early: trade for stay + tip, or modest flat fee. Raise prices when you can show **completed views in the target city niche**, not total followers.

## What not to do

- Promise “get rich as influencer” timelines
- Buy followers to look big for brands (kills deals later)
- Hard-sell every caption before trust exists
- Scatter into 5 income ideas before one content system works

## When helping

Always state which stage they’re in, what to ignore, the next money unlock, and the content work that unlocks it. Tie advice to compounding: content → reach → trust → offer → cash → more content.
